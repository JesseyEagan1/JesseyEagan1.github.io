My Journey to Coding.

But I stayed true to my word. After being accepted into RefactorU my husband and I did the courageous act of selling our home in Illinois, moving the family out West and jumping right into the world of coding by attending bootcamp.

I’m glad that I made this change for my family, and for myself - finding a career I love & thrive in, and for the software engineering field. I’m trying to improve the odds for women in coding.
